Welcome to Nakuru County Garbage Collection Management

Nakuru County Garbage Collection Management System is responsible collection of solid waste within Nakuru County Our aim is not only to provide the best solid waste collection services but to also minimize the effects caused by poor management on human health and also on the environment.

Our Service Packages

Small package
This is the cheapest package we offer. It requires you as the client to procure a 32 Gallon / 120 Liter garbage cart/bin which has 1 or 2 plastic bags of trash. Garbage will be collected from your premises on a weekly basis. You can order these garbage containers once you sign up
Charges: KShs 300 Per Month
Medium package
This is the second package we offer. It requires you as the client to procure a 64 Gallon / 240 Liter garbage cart/bin which has 3 or 4 plastic bags of trash. Garbage will be collected from your premises on a weekly basis. You can order these garbage containers once you sign up
Charges: KShs 500 Per Month
Large package
This is the maximum package we offer. It requires you as the client to procure a 95 Gallon / 360 Liter garbage cart/bin which has 5 or 6 plastic bags of trash. Garbage will be collected from your premises on a weekly basis. You can order these garbage containers once you sign up
Charges: KShs 750 Per Month
